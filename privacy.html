Your privacy matters.

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview Information we collect How we use it Data security Third-party sharing Contact privacy team

Overview

At DiagnoseThis, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of every user. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your personal data.

We built this platform with a privacy-first approach. Our mission is to empower you with health knowledge, and we believe that starts with earning your trust through transparent data practices.

Information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our services:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Health queries: Symptoms and health questions you submit through Ask WHY and related tools.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the platform, including pages visited, features used, and session duration.
  • Payment information: Billing details processed securely through our third-party payment provider. We do not store full credit card numbers.
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers for security and optimization.

How we use it

Your information is used to deliver, maintain, and improve the DiagnoseThis experience:

  • Generating personalized health explorations based on your symptoms and queries.
  • Processing payments and managing your subscription.
  • Improving our AI models and health content accuracy (using anonymized, aggregated data only).
  • Sending essential account notifications, security alerts, and service updates.
  • Providing customer support and responding to your inquiries.
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your health data is yours.

Data security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.
  • We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Multi-factor authentication is available for all accounts.
  • Automated threat detection and monitoring systems are in place around the clock.

Third-party sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted partners who assist with payment processing, hosting, and analytics, bound by strict data protection agreements.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of DiagnoseThis, our users, or the public.

We never share your individual health queries or symptom data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing purposes.

Contact privacy team

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this policy, our dedicated privacy team is here to help:

Email: privacy@diagnosethis.com

Mail: Core DT Solutions LLC, Attn: Privacy Team, P.O. Box 1234, Wilmington, DE 19801

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days. You may also request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account and all associated information at any time.
