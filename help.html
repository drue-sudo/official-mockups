Help & FAQ

Everything you need to know about DiagnoseThis.

What is DiagnoseThis?+
DiagnoseThis is an AI-powered health exploration tool built by clinicians. It helps you understand your symptoms by providing possible explanations — not diagnoses — that you can bring to your healthcare provider.
How does Ask WHY work?+
Simply type your symptom or health concern into the Ask WHY tool. Our AI, guided by clinical frameworks, generates 3 possible explanations along with questions you can ask your provider.
Is this medical advice?+
No. DiagnoseThis is strictly educational. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical advice. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider for medical decisions.
What's the difference between See WHY and The WHY?+
See WHY ($15/mo) gives you unlimited questions with detailed explanations and provider scripts. The WHY ($29/mo) adds live chat support, priority responses, and condition tracking over time.
Is my data private?+
Yes. We take privacy seriously. Your health questions are encrypted and never shared with third parties. You can delete your data at any time from your profile settings.
How do I cancel my subscription?+
You can cancel anytime from your Profile page under Subscription Plans. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

Understanding the WHY behind your health — with clarity, not conclusions.
