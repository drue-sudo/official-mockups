What is DiagnoseThis?+
DiagnoseThis is an AI-powered health exploration platform built by clinicians. It helps you understand your symptoms by generating possible explanations -- not diagnoses -- that you can discuss with your healthcare provider. Think of it as a guided starting point for better health conversations.
How does Ask WHY work?+
Type your symptom or health concern into the Ask WHY tool. Our AI, guided by clinical frameworks, analyzes your input and generates up to 3 possible explanations along with follow-up questions you can bring to your provider. The entire process takes just seconds and is designed to empower you with knowledge before your next appointment.
Is this medical advice?+
No. DiagnoseThis is strictly educational. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical advice. The explanations provided are meant to help you have more informed conversations with your licensed healthcare provider. Always consult a medical professional for health decisions.
What's the difference between See WHY and The WHY?+
See WHY ($15/mo) gives you unlimited questions with detailed explanations, provider scripts, and full access to your history. The WHY ($29/mo) includes everything in See WHY plus live chat support, priority responses, and condition tracking over time so you can monitor how your symptoms evolve.
Is my health information private?+
Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously. All health questions and data are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or share your information with third parties. You can view, export, or permanently delete your data at any time from your profile settings.
How do I cancel my subscription?+
You can cancel anytime from your Profile page under Subscription Plans. There are no cancellation fees or penalties. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period, and you can resubscribe at any time.
How many WHYs do I get for free?+
Every new account starts with 3 free WHYs so you can experience the platform before committing. Each WHY gives you a full set of explanations for one symptom or concern. After your free WHYs are used, you can upgrade to See WHY or The WHY for unlimited access.
Who builds the explanations?+
Our explanations are generated by AI models that have been fine-tuned and reviewed by a team of licensed clinicians and health educators. Every clinical framework used in the platform is vetted for accuracy, and our team continuously updates the system to reflect current medical understanding.
