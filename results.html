Your Question

Why do I get dizzy every time I stand up?

1

Orthostatic Hypotension

A sudden drop in blood pressure when standing, often caused by dehydration, medications, or nervous system conditions.

What to ask your provider

"Can we check my blood pressure lying down vs. standing?"

2

Vestibular Dysfunction

An inner ear issue that affects balance, sometimes triggered by infections, head injury, or age-related changes.

What to ask your provider

"Should I be referred to an ENT or vestibular specialist?"

3

Iron-Deficiency Anemia

Low iron levels reduce oxygen delivery to the brain, causing dizziness especially with position changes.

What to ask your provider

"Can we run a complete blood count and ferritin level?"
